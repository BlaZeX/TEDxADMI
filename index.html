About the Event

What is TEDx?

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TED has created a program called TEDx. TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to share a TED-like experience. Our event is called TEDxADMI, where x = independently organized TED event.

Theme: Genesis

This year, our theme is "Genesis" — exploring the origins of groundbreaking ideas, the dawn of new technologies, and the very beginnings of movements that shape our world. Join us as we bring together brilliant minds to share powerful ideas about the genesis of change.
